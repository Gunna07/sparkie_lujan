SCREAMS

Openings in the face of life
Reaching down and pulling out
Screams of joy and smiles abound
Only inside do I wear this frown

You see me smiling at you
Accepting what is seen to be fact
Only I know what lays within
Only my faith in God is intact

Faith surrounds those who trust
Smiles in life are an expected must
Tears never show what highways I've traveled
My lips are sealed within my faith

Can this unseen valley of trust be worthy of my silence
I think so as my heart believes in your words
Then I take it upon myself to open my doors widely
I quiver at the thought of unleashed memories

Free me from this hidden place
Let the screams of joy and love just radiate!
