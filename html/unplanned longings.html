UNPLANNED LONGINGS

You left, you said you never would!
How come you did?
You said you needed me,
Didn't you mean it?

When we touched, it was that of a lifetime,
I am longing for it again, why?
Did you know that love does hurt?
Is this what you wanted me to feel?

I protected you, and kept you warm with my ways,
Perhaps the chilling truth was, that it wasn't warm enough!
Planning to love is not what happens,
Falling in love, that is what occurred!

You love me, me loving you;
This was our plan, that was our pledge and promise!
Now it just desires and aching for those planned longings!!
