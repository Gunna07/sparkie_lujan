IS THIS WHAT WAS MEANT

Civil war? Is that what you said?
My heart tells me differently, as they all lay there dead
I wonder if killing of men is at all civil
The aching of seeing running blood hurts, even belittles

I pulled the trigger, but I don't know why
Their eyes roll back in their heads, my heart cries
I hear orders being shouted to keep shooting them down
One by one, they each slowly fall crumpling to the ground

The dirt around me is no longer brown in color, but soaked in red
Men, women, and children, my dear God so many lay in pieces dead
I do this for my country and its freedom, that is what I am told
Why dear Lord, don't I feel brave, don't I feel bold

A horrible cringing of sadness inside is all that I feel
How can I keep pulling this trigger? It can't be real
Limbs spread laying aimlessly out all over the ground
Shot up bloody bodies and pieces, and the screaming abound

Flying and explosions of flashing lights as grenades explode
The ground is shaking under my feet with each deadly blow
Dear God, is this what my country continues to call me to do
Dismembering humans is what I am ordered by you

The war is over now, and I return home
Shame on my face, my self-esteem completely blown

I wasn't a hero, I wasn't their knight in white armor

Dear God, how could my country find shame in me
