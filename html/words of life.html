WRITE ME THE WORDS OF LIFE

Write to me the words of your heart
Telling me how were growing, although miles apart
Calling me nightly, as we enjoy the exchanges of voices
Your making all the right moves, your deciding the choices

Write with a pen in your hand and a small piece of paper
The parts of your being, for my eyes to read and to savor
Share with me dreams, and your hearts tiny love spaces
Tell of your new shoes, and their sturdy white laces

Speak of the job where you spend each long day
Telling me how you do this for a living and for pay
Exchange with me the ways of your mind
Licking the envelope and addressing it to be mine

Folding the corners so nice and neat
Creasing the center, with a loving touched pleat
Finding a way to open it with such care
Adjusting my clothes and sweeping my eyes clear of my hair

Slicing it open, and unfolding the words inside
Exposing what you've written, from you hearts loving eye
Holding the unsealed letter in my hand upon my breast
Seeing the "I love you", are the only words I viewed, leaving behind the rest
