REMEMBERING WHEN

My mind takes a trip back into the past
Hoping to rekindle feelings that didn't last
We shared a childhood, so many years ago
Kicking our feet in the neighborhood pond with our toes

Eating lollipops, and giggling hours away
Promising one another, our bond would never stray
How does time separate the dreams from the reality now
Closing my eyes, remembering the smiles and your crooked eye brow

Our jeans always had holes, and our hands were always dirty
Taking our sling shots and firing at the twirling blue birdies
Skipping rounded flat rocks over the pond in a race
Trying to see if they would skips over the waves with grace

Do you remember the bikes we both painted
Mom got so angry, she yelled and yelled till she almost fainted
Giggling right now as I remember these images
Lying together on the grass during the football scrimmages

The neighborhood girls, would gather behind a huge tree
Watching us frolic and play, hoping we couldn't see
All of them standing their pretty, with frills and envious eyes
Only you and me knew they were looking, cause we were the spies

Those were the days, my mind loves to remember
Also the Christmas's we shared in the month of December
Where are you now as the days slip away, oh buddy of mine
I put away my boyish behavior but would love to share just one more time

If you miss the days that we traveled together back then
I suggest you close your eyes and see these images and pretend
Us meeting again and having such a wonderful time on this day
I am a woman now, wondering where my childhood buddy is at play
